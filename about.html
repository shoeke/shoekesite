About Me

I've always enjoyed looking at art and making things since I was a young girl. I ended up majoring in art at a liberal arts university in California. It wasn't exactly art school and did not leave me prepared for any kind of career in art. I did less and less "art" as I tried to find my place in the working world and did end up doing a bit of graphic design and desktop publishing (remember that term?!) among other marketing related duties at various tech startups. After a couple of years I ended up getting married and being a stay at home mom to 3 kids. Doing crafts and projects with my children I slowly started doing art again but this time from a craft and enjoyment approach, rather than a fine arts approach. Fun is better is than perfect! I've been combining my love of the computer and love of drawing to come up with happy go-betweens. I'm still evolving and hope to never stop!
